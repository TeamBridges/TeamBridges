Lenape Body Parts Glossary

A comprehensive list of Lenape vocabulary for body parts organized by category.

Upper Body

  • ahshilunkon - armpit
  • ahshilunkona - armpits
  • hopikon - shoulder
  • hopikona - shoulders
  • mixeken - body hair/fur
  • mixekena - pl body hair/fur
  • mutay - stomach, abdomen
  • mutaya - stomachs, abdomens
  • naolk - arm
  • naolka - arms
  • wiskon - elbow
  • wiskona - elbows

Lower Body

  • hwikat - leg
  • hwikata - legs
  • hwikxkon - shin
  • hwikxkona - shins
  • ketukw - knee
  • kwetko - knees
  • pom - thigh
  • poma - thighs
  • tankkat - ankle
  • tankkata - ankles

Hands and Feet

  • hwikash - fingernail, toenail
  • hwikasha - fingernails, toenails
  • kwetsit - toe
  • kwetsita - toes
  • naxk - hand
  • naxka - hands
  • tukwelench - fist
  • tukwelencha - fists
  • welench - finger
  • welencha - fingers
  • wsit - foot
  • wsita - feet

Head and Neck

  • hwitaok - ear
  • hwitaoka - ears
  • hwikwi - chin
  • hwikwia - chins
  • mamaon - eyebrow
  • mamaona - eyebrows
  • milaxk - human hair
  • shetun - lip
  • shetuna - lips
  • tun - mouth
  • tuna - mouths
  • wananu - cheek
  • wananuwa - cheeks
  • weshkinkw - face
  • weshkinko - faces
  • wikiyon - nose
  • wikiyona - noses
  • wil - head
  • wila - heads
  • wipit - tooth
  • wipita - teeth
  • xkwekankan - neck
  • xkwekankana - necks

Bones and Internal Organs

  • hopan - lungs
  • hopanak - lungs
  • mhukw - blood
  • teh - heart
  • teha - hearts
  • tuhwepi - body
  • tuhwepia - bodies
  • welakwshi - intestine
  • welakwshia - intestines
  • xkan - bone
  • xkana - bones
  • xkelixen - rib
  • xkelixena - ribs
  • xkwen - liver
  • xkwena - livers
