Lenape Drag & Drop

Match Lenape words with their meanings!

How to Play:

  • Click on a Lenape word and drag it to its matching body part
  • Click again to check your answer
  • Easy Mode: Check each word as you go
  • Challenge Mode: Match all the words, then check your answers when you're done
