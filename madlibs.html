How to Play Lenape Mad Libs

  • Choose a story template from the options on the left
  • Fill in the blanks with the requested Lenape words
  • Use the word examples on the right for help with vocabulary
  • Click "Generate Story" to see your completed story
  • Words in parentheses (like this) show the English translation
  • Click "Create Another Story" to start over with a new story
  • Try different combinations of words to create unique stories!
